ABOUT

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The worlds oldest question: all work and no play, or all play and no work? So many of us have always struggled to find the correct balance between self-care and productivity. This has inhibited and concealed so much hidden potential across the globe. That's why Coup De Vie is delivering kits, around the globe, to help you achieve success, and amelioration. Our kits contain a range of items, which you can customise, to aid your self-care and reflection.

The common denominator between all successful people is internal reflection. With every kit, no matter how small or big, you receive free Coup De Vie journal cards. They contain questions and prompts to discover what you need to achieve your dreams through your subconscious, and exclusive CDV advice from those on Forbes 30 under 30, past The Apprentice winners and big names in several industries.

WORKER'S RIGHTS

We rampantly call for all our workers to be unionize, and protect all worker's rights. We will soon be supporting other unions around the world, especially in under-developed countries.

SUSTAINABILITY

We promise to be sustainable, which most importantly includes our packaging. We use cardboard and paper packaging. Our totes are an alternative to plastic bags. However, as a small business, there is only so much change we can make. Currently, we don't have a choice but to use mostly fossil fuels. Hence, why we support and advocate for change in the fundamentals of our electricity, and to immediately make the swap to renewables.
